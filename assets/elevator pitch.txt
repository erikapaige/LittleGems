Elevator pitch
Hole in the Wall
simple tool to find good restaurants very close to you
that doesn't get the customers it deserves

uses location api to figure out where you are and finds 
restaurants nearby that have good reviews (4 stars or higher) 
but doesn't have a lot of reviews  (100 reviews or less). 
Then it shows you the best.

Mozilla location api
yelp api
yelp gives a $200 credit and subtracts $5 for every 1000
locations you use
